SUPPORT

Need help? We have developers and data scientists too

    GCC - UMCG

    Primary MOLGENIS support is currently coordinated by the Genomics Coordination Center (GCC) hosted at the Dept of Genetics of University Medical Center Groningen. GCC provides hosting of MOLGENIS services, project based development and routine analysis.

    BBMRI & TraIT

    Also biobank research infrastructure BBMRI-NL and translational research infrastructure CTMM-TraIT provide hosting support for MOLGENIS.

    More information at http://bbmri.nl and http://www.ctmm-trait.nl/
